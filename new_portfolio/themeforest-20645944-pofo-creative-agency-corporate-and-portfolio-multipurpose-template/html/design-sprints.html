How might Cvent implement Design Sprints?

Cvent | Product Designer | 9.17-present
Design Sprints Facilitation
Summary

As a large company with a 20 year history, Cvent is interested in Design Sprints to infuse collaborative, rapid problem solving and prototyping. I am part of the a team that is investigating what sorts of product problems at Cvent are appropriate for Design Sprints, putting together materials to help our teams run Design Sprints, and piloting our program. I am also serving as a facilitator in Sprints. Due to confidentiality, I cannot disclose the materials or topics of Cvent Design Sprints here.
